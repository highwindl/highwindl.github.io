# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 2026-01-18

**DreamSoftware** ("we", "our") operates the **Embedded Helper** app. This page is used to inform visitors regarding our policies with the collection, use, and disclosure of Personal Information if anyone decided to use our Service on the **Google Play Store** or **Apple App Store**.

### 1. Information Collection & Usage
We do not collect any personal information directly from our users on our servers. 

The app processes the following data locally on your device:
* **Generated Code:** C Structures from JSON, Font Arrays from Dot Matrix drawings.
* **User Inputs:** Hex/Bin/Dec values, RGB color selections.

All conversions and code generation happen locally on your device's processor. The generated code is strictly for your use and is not transmitted to us.

### 2. Device Permissions
* **Clipboard Access:** The app provides functionality to copy generated code (e.g., C Arrays, Structs) to your system clipboard for use in other applications (IDEs, Text Editors).
* **Internet Access:** Required only for loading advertisements (AdMob) and processing In-App Purchases.

### 3. Third-Party Services
Our app uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you for advertising, analytics, and payment processing purposes.

We use the following third-party services:

* **Google AdMob:** Used to display advertisements.
* **Google Play Services:** Used for core app functionality on Android.
* **Google Play Billing & Apple In-App Purchase:** Used for processing "Remove Ads" transactions.

### 4. Log Data
We want to inform you that whenever you use our Service, in a case of an error in the app, we collect data and information (through third-party products) on your phone called Log Data. This Log Data may include information such as your device IP address, device name, operating system version, and the time/date of your use.

### 5. Children’s Privacy
These Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

### 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
