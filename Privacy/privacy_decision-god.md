# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 2026-01-14

**DreamSoftware** ("we", "our") operates the **Decision God (결정의 신)** mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our App on Android (Google Play) and iOS (Apple App Store).

### 1. Information Collection and Use

**1.1 Personal Data**
We do not knowingly collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (such as your name, address, phone number, or email address) on our servers.

**1.2 Local Storage**
The App stores game settings, history, and preferences locally on your device (using Shared Preferences on Android and UserDefaults on iOS). This data is stored solely on your device and is not transmitted to us. **Uninstalling the App will result in the deletion of this locally stored data.**

**1.3 Financial Data (In-App Purchases)**
Our App offers In-App Purchases (Premium features). We do not process or store your payment information (e.g., credit card numbers). All payment transactions are handled directly by the respective app store platforms:
* **Apple App Store (iOS):** Processed by Apple Inc.
* **Google Play Store (Android):** Processed by Google LLC.

### 2. Third-Party Services and Data Tracking

We use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you for the purpose of serving personalized advertisements and analytics.

**2.1 Advertising**
We use **Google AdMob** to display ads. AdMob may use device identifiers (such as the Advertising ID on Android or IDFA on iOS) to show ads that are relevant to you.
* **iOS Users:** In accordance with Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework, the App will request your permission to track your activity across other companies' apps and websites for advertising purposes. You can decline this request or manage it in your iOS Settings.

**2.2 Service Providers**
Links to privacy policies of third-party service providers used by the App:
* [Google AdMob & AdSense](https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads)
* [Google Play Services](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
* [Apple Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/)

### 3. Log Data
We want to inform you that whenever you use our Service, in a case of an error in the app, we collect data and information (through third-party products like Google Play Services or Apple crash reports) on your phone called Log Data. This Log Data may include information such as your device Internet Protocol ("IP") address, device name, operating system version, the configuration of the app when utilizing our Service, the time and date of your use of the Service, and other statistics.

### 4. Children’s Privacy
These Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

### 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
