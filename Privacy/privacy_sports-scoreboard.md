# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 2026-01-14

**DreamSoftware** ("we", "our") operates the **Sports Scoreboard (Universal Scoreboard)** app (available on **Google Play Store** and **Apple App Store**) and this website.

### 1. Information Collection
We do not collect any personal information directly from our users. 
However, the app may store **game settings, team names, and score history** locally on your device (using Shared Preferences or UserDefaults), which is not shared with us or any third parties.

### 2. Third-Party Services
Our app uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you for advertising, analytics, and payment processing purposes.

We use the following third-party services:

* **Google AdMob:** Used to display advertisements.
* **Google Play Services (Android):** Used for core app functionality on Android devices.
* **Google Play Billing (Android):** Used for processing In-App Purchases (Premium Upgrade) on Android. We do not store or have access to your credit card information or financial data; these are handled securely by Google.
* **Apple Services (iOS):** Used for core app functionality on iOS devices.
* **Apple In-App Purchase (iOS):** Used for processing In-App Purchases (Premium Upgrade) on iOS. We do not store or have access to your credit card information or financial data; these are handled securely by Apple.

### 3. Log Data
We want to inform you that whenever you use our Service, in a case of an error in the app, we collect data and information (through third-party products) on your phone called Log Data. This Log Data may include information such as your device Internet Protocol (“IP”) address, device name, operating system version, the configuration of the app when utilizing our Service, the time and date of your use of the Service, and other statistics.

### 4. Children’s Privacy
These Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.
